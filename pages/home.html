Overview

Riser Africa foundation is an organization whose main purpose is to empower African people especially youth and women the the fight against poverty. According to the world bank, poverty refers to those who have less than 1.25 US dollars a day to live, and thus live on the edge of existence. Extreme poverty leads to hunger in Africa: More than a quarter of the hunger in the world live on African continent. Taking a deeper look into poverty we will realize that poverty is much more than lack of money. It’s not strictly about measuring household income, income inequality or the need for economic growth. Poverty changes everything about a person’s life experience. It impacts all of a person’s basic needs and touches every aspect of his /her existence.
